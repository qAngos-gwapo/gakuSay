My Hopes

If you haven’t read my learnings on online class, I highly reccomend you to read it first before reading this article, as I will now be talking about my hopes and wishes for next school year.

My first and maybe most important wish is to bring back face to face classes. This is because it will help students learn more and give them a reason to study.

Secondly, if face to face classes won’t return any time soon, I hope internet speeds will become faster and cheaper in the country. This is so that everyone can go to school at minimal cost per month, knowing that we are a third world country.

Finally, I hope workloads will be lightened next school year, so that school will not be a factor that affect a student’s mental health.
